Email Signature — Copy/Paste Instructions

  1. Edit your details directly in the rendered signature below (click the text and edit).
  2. Click anywhere inside the dashed box, then press Cmd+A (or Ctrl+A) to select.
  3. Copy with Cmd+C (or Ctrl+C).
  4. Paste into your email client's signature settings (Gmail: Settings → General → Signature).
  5. Replace the email and phone with your real details before saving.
