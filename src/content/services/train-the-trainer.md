## Build capacity that sustains beyond our engagement

The most effective professional development programs don't depend on external providers forever. Train-the-Trainer engagements are designed to transfer our expertise to your internal team — coaches, coordinators, department leads, and teacher-leaders — so they can deliver high-quality PD independently.

## What you get

Each Train-the-Trainer module includes:

- **Facilitator guides** — Detailed, session-by-session guides with timing, talking points, facilitation notes, and troubleshooting tips
- **Participant materials** — Ready-to-use handouts, activity sheets, and digital resources for participants
- **Assessment rubrics** — Tools for evaluating participant understanding and engagement during and after sessions
- **Certification training** — We train your facilitators directly, model the sessions, co-facilitate initial deliveries, and certify readiness

## How it works

We develop each module in close collaboration with your team, ensuring the content reflects your district's context, standards, and goals. The development process typically includes:

1. **Co-design** — We work with your team to define learning objectives, select content, and design activities
2. **Pilot delivery** — We deliver the module to your first cohort, with your facilitators observing
3. **Co-facilitation** — Your facilitators lead the next delivery with our support
4. **Independent delivery** — Your team runs the module independently, with our feedback available as needed

## Topics available

Train-the-Trainer modules can be developed for any of our core topic areas, including STEM integration, AI literacy, computational thinking, and data science. Each module is designed for groups of three or more participants per activity.
