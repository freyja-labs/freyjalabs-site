## Same philosophy, flexible format

Not every engagement requires an on-site visit. Our Virtual & Hybrid sessions deliver the same hands-on, active-participation philosophy in formats that work for distributed teams, tight schedules, and broader reach.

## Formats we offer

- **Live virtual sessions** — 90-minute to full-day interactive sessions via video conference, with breakout groups, collaborative activities, and real-time facilitation
- **Asynchronous modules** — Self-paced learning modules with embedded activities, reflection prompts, and practical application tasks
- **Hybrid models** — Combination of asynchronous preparation with live virtual or in-person sessions for deeper engagement
- **Virtual coaching** — One-on-one or small-group coaching sessions for instructional leaders implementing new content areas

## What makes our virtual PD different

Most virtual PD is a webinar with a Q&A. Ours isn't. We design for active participation even in remote formats:

- Breakout activities for groups of three or more participants
- Collaborative digital workspaces where teams build artifacts in real time
- Structured reflection and peer feedback cycles
- Application tasks that connect directly to participants' classrooms

## When to choose virtual

Virtual & Hybrid formats are ideal when you need:

- **Broader reach** — Train teachers across multiple buildings or regions without travel
- **Flexible scheduling** — Fit PD into existing professional learning time
- **Follow-up support** — Add virtual coaching or async modules after an on-site engagement
- **Budget efficiency** — Access Freyja Labs expertise at a lower per-session cost
